US Department of Labor

The assignment was to review a government website in need of an enhanced user experience.

In partnership with another UX/UI developer, there was a complete evaluation of the Department of Labor's website. It was quickly determined that the website was not mobile responsive and was in dire need of reorganization and reframing of information. Skills used to complete project: Heuristic Evaluation, User Research, Analysis, Interaction design and prototyping in Figma and User testing.
